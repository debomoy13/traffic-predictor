Our Story

Urban traffic is unpredictable and stressful. We started Traffy Timey with a simple idea — people should know the best time to leave before they step out. By observing daily congestion patterns, we aim to turn uncertainty into informed decisions.

Like many commuters, we experienced the frustration of leaving on time yet arriving late due to sudden traffic buildup. Existing navigation tools often react to traffic once you are already on the road, leaving little room to plan ahead.

Traffy Timey was created to shift this mindset from reaction to preparation. By focusing on patterns rather than momentary conditions, we aim to help people make calmer, smarter travel choices — before their journey even begins.

Our Solution

Traffy Timey analyzes historical traffic trends and time-based patterns to estimate how long a journey may take. Instead of reacting to traffic, our system helps users plan ahead and choose smarter departure times.

Traffic conditions often follow repeatable patterns influenced by time of day, day of the week, and common commuting behaviors. By studying these recurring trends, Traffy Timey focuses on prediction rather than real-time reaction.

This approach allows users to make informed decisions before leaving, reducing unnecessary delays and stress. Our goal is not just to show routes, but to provide clarity — helping people understand *when* to travel, not just *where*.

Upcoming Features

We are working towards route-level insights, city-specific optimization, and real-time adaptability. Future versions will integrate smarter predictions and broader coverage for urban commuters.
